About Me
I’m a data scientist with a passion for uncovering meaningful insights from complex datasets. With a strong foundation in statistical analysis, machine learning, and visualization, I enjoy crafting solutions that make data actionable and impactful. With a background in Genetics, I have a particular affinity for biological data.
In my free time I love reading, watching movies, hiking and playing 5-a-side football. I love databases of all kinds - I can't help it. You can find my Letterboxd here and a catalogue of books I have recently read here. In 2024, I took some time off and did some travelling, which resulted in two travel pieces written for Fairlady Magazine and a near-fatal pastry addiction. I am based in Cape Town, South Africa.
